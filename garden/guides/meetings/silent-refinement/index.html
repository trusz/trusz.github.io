Silent Refinement

info

In the following I assume a basic understanding the Refinement ↗ meeting.

What is the goal?​

This refinement method aims to promote a better understanding of users-contexts by allowing each team member to read, digest and understand the problems at her/his pace without peer pressure.

A secondary goal is to describe the tickets so good that:

  1. they don't need any clarification
  2. any question can be answered in the Phase 1

How does the method look like?​

The Core​

The core phases are important to achieve our goal.

Phase 1 - Silent Refinement​
  1. The team silently reads the stories for 30 minutes.
  2. If you have a question, you can write it down
  3. If you know the answer, answer the question
  4. If you don't have any more questions and you could vote, signalize it
  5. Go to the next story and repeat
Phase 2 - Quick Vote​
  1. Take all the stories that have no questions and everybody understood them.
  2. Let the team vote
  3. Document the final vote
Phase 3 - Slow Vote​
  1. Take the rest of the stories with questions and discuss them one by one
  2. Let the team vote
  3. Document the final vote

Addons​

The following add-ons are not necessary, but can help to reach our secondary goal or any additional improvements.

Refinement Feedback​

This is a quick but inaccurate feedback module. After "Phase 3" we gather scores (1-5) from everybody how good the refinement was. This allows us to monitor the quality of refinement with very low costs.

tip

We can have follow up question or we can ask for additional feedback if the quality has greatly changed

Story Feedback​

Similar to "Refinement Feedback", but on a story level. With this add-on we can find out which descriptions worked and which did not

tip

You can try out different improvements and see which worked better

Variants​

The following changes to the core can adjust the refinement for your team's need.

Increase or Decrease Time Limit​

The 30 minutes is experience based, meaning it has worked well in the past. You can adjust this limit but keep in mind the following:

  • with not enough time, people cannot read and interpret and ask questions about descriptions good enough and that leads to a long "Phase 3 - Slow Vote", which is just the same as the old format
  • with too much time written discussion can become cumbersome. Having the discussion written down is good for individual thinking and documentations, however sometimes a quick chat maybe can clear up misunderstandings

1-by-1​

Go through all the phases with each task separately instead of taking the time limit and read all tasks.

Advantages:

  • you just read the story so you still remember the details for further discussions and for voting

Disadvantages:

  • some may get bored if they are quicker
  • it can cause pressure to move quicker and understand less of the context
